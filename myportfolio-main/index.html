GSR Description

Humans are bound by their feelings. These feelings can be stress, anger, happiness, excitement, etc. But these feelings can also be so dangerous when it exceeds. So, to detect the feeling inside the people, a device has been designed to monitor them continuously and can even know their state. By monitoring all of these, suggestions can be made to them to overcome their emotions. Galvanic Skin Response (GSR), is used to analyse the stress level using the skin’s electrical activity in a non-invasive manner. Human body is processing all the information in the form of signals. The electrical signal throughout the body will be the same. If a person undergoes any emotions parallelly electrical activity will also have a change in their body. Especially in ICU/OT, the patient undergoes emotional ambivalence. They may be stressed or afraid. The patients don’t tell easily what they are feeling. So, the main purpose of this device is to identify the emotions of an individual from their skin resistance. This can be done by connecting electrodes to the skin from the device. When the electrode is placed on the skin it detects the conductance, resistance. By using the conductance and resistance value the stress level will be calculated and displayed on the display.
